# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and inclusive
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or threatening language of any kind.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Disruptive behaviour in issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project
maintainers at `conduct@example.invalid`. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly.
